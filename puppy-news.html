Unicorn puppy found abandoned!

The unicorn puppy, affectionately called Narwhal, was found abandoned with a secondary tail on his head. The extra tail is said to cause no pain for the puppy but is a very cute addition to an already adorable face. The tail is about a third of the size of his actual tail and does not stop him from running around and playing for hours.

Puppies play the Puppy Bowl!

Fed up of American Football? Want something cuter? Why not watch the Puppy Bowl?!

The adorable alternative programme has been broadcasted to screens for the past 10 years. The show comes complete with a kitty halftime show and penguin cheerleaders - what more could a viewer want?! 10 puppies fight it out to drag a chew toy into the end zone to score a touchdown.
